Coefficients

The mononumeric coefficient is the number of mononumeric sets that you find divided by the total number of sets that you find. The monochromatic, monoshapic, and monoshadic coefficients are similar. Note that if you pick sets uniformly at random, then your coefficients will approach 1/3.

You must have found at least 10 sets, in games that get played to completion, to appear here.
